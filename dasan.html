But you redefined those boundaries.

I believe that in a way, we are platonic soulmates. We were never meant to be romantic. We were just meant to be. Although the love we have for each other is undeniable, and although the love we have for each other exists, it was not meant to stretch beyond the pure unions of friendship. It was not meant to stretch at our heart strings, bur rather, it was meant to gently massage them into peace. It was not meant to stretch past the boundaries we subconsciously set to ensure that things don't become complicated. It was not meant to stretch our connection into emotional destinations that have no return ticket if we took that plunge. My love for you is pure, no motive or obligation attached to it. So is it so hard to believe that our paths were perfectly aligned to meet and we were placed in each other's lives even if it wasn't for a 'romantic' purpose? Is it so hard to fathom that a fondness can exist between two people whose only intention is pouring the love that exists between them back into a friendship? I don't think so.
